Department of Housing Preservation and Development (HPD)

Violations against conditions in rental dwelling units that have been verified to violate the New York City Housing Maintenance Code (HMC) or the New York State Multiple Dwelling Law (MDL). These violations can result in inspections, fees and other corrective requirements. Additionally, excessive violations may result in a lien on the property and make it more difficult to obtain financing. More info
